TOP RESEARCH

Tooth Regeneration Advances

Tooth regeneration, a stem cell-based approach, aims to regrow teeth from autologous stem cells. The first clinical trial for a tooth regrowth medicine began in Japan in 2023, marking a significant milestone in regenerative dentistry.

Fluoride Treatments Post-Cleaning

Dental professionals often recommend fluoride treatments after cleanings to strengthen enamel and prevent cavities. These treatments are particularly beneficial for individuals at high risk of tooth decay, although insurance coverage may vary.

Hydroxyapatite in Dentistry

Hydroxyapatite, especially in its nano form, is gaining attention for its potential to counteract dentine hypersensitivity and aid in enamel remineralization. Studies suggest its effectiveness in reducing sensitivity and preventing cavities, although its widespread adoption in dental practice is still under consideration.
